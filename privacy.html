Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

NibbleFind (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the NibbleFind mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email address and authentication details)
  • Location data (to show nearby vendors)
  • Vendor-submitted information (business name, photos, address, hours)
  • User ratings and favorites

How We Use Information

  • To display vendor locations on the map
  • To allow users to save favorites and rate vendors
  • To provide account and subscription services
  • To improve app functionality and performance

Location Data

Location is used only to display nearby vendors and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Data Storage

Data is securely stored using Firebase services.

Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time within the app under Profile → Delete Account.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@nibblefind.com.
