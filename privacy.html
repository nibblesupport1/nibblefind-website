Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

NibbleFind ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the NibbleFind mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials.

Location Information

If you grant permission, we collect location data to display nearby vendors and improve your experience within the app.

Vendor-Submitted Information

Vendors may submit business information including name, address, schedule, phone number, photos, and descriptions.

User Activity

We collect information related to favorites, ratings, and interactions within the app.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display vendor locations on the map
  • To allow users to save favorites and rate vendors
  • To manage user accounts
  • To provide subscription services (if applicable)
  • To improve app functionality and performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is securely stored using Google Cloud infrastructure, including Firebase services. We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data.

4. Location Data

Location data is used solely to provide map-based features and is not sold to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by trusted service providers solely for the purpose of operating the app.

6. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time by navigating to Profile → Delete Account within the app. Upon deletion, the following data is permanently removed: - Account information (name, email) - Favorites and ratings - Profile data If you have uninstalled the app and need to request account deletion, please contact us at support@nibblefind.com.

7. Children's Privacy

NibbleFind is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@nibblefind.com
